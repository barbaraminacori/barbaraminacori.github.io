My goal in dance is to create a physical and honest movement material. It is essential for me to focus on the naturalness and the purity of the meaning of a movement. Being a dancer means that my body and my mind have merged and grown together at the same level. Only at that point it will come natural for myself to express every single value in which I believe in. During a creative process my aim is to look inside myself and try to comprehend the emotions that bring certain behaviour patterns with them. Human behaviour and states of mind are my principal sources of inspirations. 

Barbara. M
